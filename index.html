AI PE Playground

AI PE Lab

A browser-first set of physical education demos built with MediaPipe Pose and Three.js. The current release focuses on one polished example: a three-stage adventure that turns body movement into a runner, rower, and reef-jump challenge.

Three-Stage Adventure

A classroom-friendly playground with three linked motion tasks: forest running, river rowing, and reef hopping.

Open by Design

The repo is structured so teachers and developers can adapt thresholds, scenes, and UI copy for their own classes.
